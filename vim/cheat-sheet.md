# Vim - Cheat Sheet

### Yank

#### Yank string
`2yi"` / `2yi'`

#### Yank within parantheses
`yi)`

#### Yank within brackets
`yi]`

#### Toggle fold
`za`

#### Visually select paragraph
`vap`


### Window

#### Maximize vertically
`ctrl+W` `_`

#### Maximize horizontally
`ctrl+W` `|`

#### Restore to even sizes
`ctrl+W` `=`

### Tabs

#### Open buffer in new split
`:tab split`

#### Close tab
`:tabc`

## Case

#### Toggle case
`g~`
